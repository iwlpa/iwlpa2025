Overview

Join us for the International Workshop on Leavitt Path Algebras (IWLPA), hosted at North-Eastern Hill University (NEHU), Shillong, from April 7th to 11th, 2025, and jointly organized by NEHU and the Centre for Research in Mathematics and Data Science, Western Sydney University.

This workshop will explore the vibrant field of Leavitt Path Algebras (LPAs), focusing on K-theoretic tools in LPAs and Representation theoretic tools in LPAs.

Tailored for graduate students, early-career researchers, and established mathematicians, the event offers a blend of introductory lectures, problem-solving sessions, and opportunities for interdisciplinary discussion.

We invite you to Shillong for an inspiring week of learning, collaboration, and mathematical discovery!
