Overview

The International Workshop on Leavitt Path Algebras (IWLPA) will be held at North-Eastern Hill University (NEHU), Shillong, from April 7th to 11th, 2025. The event is jointly organized by NEHU and the Centre for Research in Mathematics and Data Science, Western Sydney University.

This workshop will explore K-theoretic tools in LPAs and Representation theoretic tools in LPAs. Tailored for graduate students, early-career researchers, and established mathematicians, it offers a blend of lectures, problem-solving sessions, and interdisciplinary discussions.
