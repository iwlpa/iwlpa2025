Registration Form

Deadline for registration: 20th March 2025 (for Indian participants); 1st March 2025 (for international participants).

Participants are requested to upload the following documents:

1. A CV (Curriculum Vitae).
2. A motivation letter (in approximately 300 words) explaining why you want to attend the workshop.
3. (Optional) Abstract in about 300 words: For participants interested in delivering a research talk on topics related to the workshop themes.

Category 1: Bachelor/ Master/ PhD student participant.

1. Registration Fees: Nil.
2. Shared accommodation will be arranged in NEHU guest house for free.
3. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner will be arranged in NEHU guest house for selected participants on payment of 700 INR/day (9 USD/day).

Category 2: Other selected participant (Including faculties and Postdoctoral fellows).

1. Registration Fees: Nil.
2. Accommodation will not be provided by the organizers. However, if notified in advance, the organizers can arrange accommodation at the NEHU Guest House, subject to the payment of the applicable room rates.
3. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner will be arranged in NEHU guest house for selected participants on payment of 700 INR/day (9 USD/day).



Note: Participants in both Category 1 and Category 2 are required to make payments for meals/accommodation directly to the NEHU Guest House official. Detailed payment instructions and applicable rates for accommodation will be provided separately to the participants in advance.
